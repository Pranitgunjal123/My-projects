Akshay Kumar is an Indian-born Canadian actor and film producer who works in Hindi films. He has appeared in over 150 films and has won several awards, including the National Film Award for Best Actor for his performance in Rustom (2016) and two Filmfare Awards. Born in Punjab, India, Kumar moved to Bangkok, Thailand, where he worked as a chef and a waiter before pursuing a career in acting. He made his debut in the film Saugandh (1991) and gained recognition with the action film Khiladi (1992). Over the years, he has established himself as one of the leading actors in Bollywood, known for his versatility and dedication to fitness.
